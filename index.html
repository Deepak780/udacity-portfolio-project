About

Hi, I'm Brian Haughey and I am an aspiring web developer currently taking my first Udacity Nanodegree program -- Front End Web Development.

I currently have two businesses. The first is BH Woodworking, which is a small custom cabinetry shop located in Lakemoor, IL. We build, finish and install custom cabinetry for clients in the Chicagoland area.

My second business is Cabinetshop Maestro which provides a project management software system to fellow custom cabinetmakers.

I have always loved computers and I am mostly self-taught. I am very much looking forward to this Front End Nanodegree (and hopefully others after) to help provide me with more experience and knowledge for future endeavors and opportunities.
